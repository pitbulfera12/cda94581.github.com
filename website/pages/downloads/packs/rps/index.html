Chunk Borders Resource Pack
Creator: LinusDev

The ChunkBounds Resource Pack shows chunk borders in your Minecraft Bedrock World! This is useful for many farms, as you sometimes need to chunk align them. I also have this constantly on his Blasting to Bedrock world, as that is how I have all the chunks
Note: As of Windows 10 1.16.200+, shaders do not work, including chunk borders
